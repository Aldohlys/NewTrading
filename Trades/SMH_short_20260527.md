## 7. Why This Trade vs Doing Nothing

A negative-EV trade only makes sense as a **portfolio convexity buy.** Three justifications specific to this position:

1. **Hedges the implicit long beta** the rest of the book carries via vol-selling and breakout positions. If the parabolic semi top is in, broader risk-off follows — this spread helps in that scenario.
2. **Size is bounded** at \$276 — uncorrelated lottery tickets at <1% account risk are a legitimate tactical sleeve, not a position-sizing breach.
3. **The asymmetry is real.** 2.6R payoff means even at 30% hit-rate the strategy is +EV repeated. Single trade is convex; the *frequency* of taking similar setups (parabolic + IVP high + first red day) is what makes the lifetime expectancy work.

If the trade hits \$0 at expiry, no narrative correction needed — that was priced into the −\$26 EV.
