# Airbus Company Insights

## Business Sectors & Segments

Airbus operates through **three main divisions**:

1. **Commercial Aircraft** (~60% of revenue)
   - €33.9 billion in 9M 2025 (up 3% YoY)
   - Largest revenue contributor
   - Focus on narrow-body and wide-body aircraft

2. **Airbus Helicopters** (~10% of revenue)
   - €5.7 billion in 9M 2025 (up 16% YoY)
   - Strong growth in services and programs

3. **Airbus Defence and Space** (~20% of revenue)
   - €8.9 billion in 9M 2025 (up 17% YoY)
   - Higher volumes across all business lines

4. **Services** (~10% of revenue)
   - Growing aftermarket and support services

## Financial Performance (2025)

**Nine-Month Results (Latest)**
- Revenue: €47.4 billion (up 7% YoY)
- Net Income: €2.64 billion (vs. €1.81 billion in 9M 2024)
- EPS: €3.34 (vs. €2.29 in 9M 2024)
- Free Cash Flow: -€914 million (reflecting inventory build-up for Q4 deliveries)

**Full Year 2025 Guidance**
- Target deliveries: ~820 commercial aircraft
- Expected EBIT: ~€7 billion
- Free Cash Flow target: ~€4.5 billion

**Key Challenges**
- Supply chain tensions (especially narrow-body engines)
- Tariff impact: €100-200 million for full year
- Production ramp-up pressures

## Revenue Streams
